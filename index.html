Know Me

Call me a go-getter. Call me resourceful. Call me a voice for change in farming.

Hello, I’m Pratyush—an agritech entrepreneur with a relentless passion for reshaping agriculture through AI and cutting-edge technology. I believe in solving real-world challenges using advanced technology and collaboration. From optimizing yield and sustainability to addressing the nuanced needs of smallholder farmers, my work is rooted in a vision of agriculture that values both innovation and inclusivity. Driven, innovative, and ready to empower, I’m here to create sustainable, impactful solutions that uplift the very backbone of our society—the farmers.
